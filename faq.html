Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking "Forgot your details?" on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.

How can I view my policies?

After logging in to your MyAviva account, you can view all your policies in the dashboard under "Your policies".

How do I make a claim?

You can submit a claim through your dashboard or contact our customer support team for assistance.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and financial information.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the contact form on this website or call our customer service line.
